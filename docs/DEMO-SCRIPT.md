# RetailMind AI - Demo Script (2-3 minutes)

## Opening (15 seconds)
"Hi, I'm presenting RetailMind AI - an AI-powered market intelligence platform that helps small retailers make smarter pricing and inventory decisions using AWS services."

---

## Problem Statement (20 seconds)
"Small retailers struggle with:
- Manual competitor price monitoring
- Reactive pricing decisions
- Inventory stockouts and overstock
- Limited access to market intelligence

This costs them revenue and competitive advantage."

---

## Solution Overview (20 seconds)
"RetailMind AI solves this with 5 AI-powered features:
1. AI Copilot for natural language queries
2. Automated competitor price monitoring
3. Smart recommendation engine
4. Proactive alert system
5. Analytics dashboard for tracking impact"

---

## Live Demo (90 seconds)

### 1. Command Center - AI Copilot (20s)
**Action:** Navigate to Command Center
**Say:** "Our AI Copilot, powered by Amazon Nova Pro, answers business questions in natural language."
**Demo:** Type "What products need attention today?"
**Show:** AI provides detailed analysis with specific numbers and recommendations

### 2. Insights - Product Overview (15s)
**Action:** Navigate to Insights
**Say:** "We monitor 5 products with over ₹1 million in inventory value."
**Show:** Product cards with real-time pricing and stock levels

### 3. Decisions - Smart Recommendations (20s)
**Action:** Navigate to Decisions page
**Say:** "Our recommendation engine analyzes market data and generates actionable suggestions."
**Demo:** Click "Generate Recommendations"
**Show:** AI-generated recommendations with confidence scores and expected impact

### 4. Alerts - Proactive Monitoring (15s)
**Action:** Navigate to Alerts
**Say:** "The alert system proactively notifies retailers of critical market changes."
**Show:** Real-time alerts categorized by severity (price drops, stock risks, opportunities)

### 5. Outcomes - Impact Tracking (20s)
**Action:** Navigate to Outcomes
**Say:** "Retailers can track the revenue impact of every decision they implement."
**Show:** Decision history with before/after metrics and ₹1,746 revenue impact
