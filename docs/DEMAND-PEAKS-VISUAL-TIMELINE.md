## 💰 Profit Optimization Tips by Season

### High Multiplier Periods (2.0x+)
```
DIWALI (2.5x) - 6 Week Cycle
├─ Weeks 1-2 (Oct 1-14):   Stock 3x normal → Price +20%
├─ Weeks 3-4 (Oct 15-28):  Stock 4x normal → Price +10%  
├─ Week 5 (Oct 29-Nov 4):  PEAK - Stock 5x → Price +5%
└─ Weeks 6-7 (Nov 5-18):   Discount 10-20% to clear

HOLI (2.1x) - 3 Week Cycle
├─ Week 1 (Mar 1-7):  Stock 2x normal → Price normal
├─ Week 2 (Mar 8-13): Stock 3x normal → Price +10%
└─ Week 3 (Mar 14-20): PEAK - Discount 5-15%

CHRISTMAS (2.2x) - 4 Week Cycle
├─ Week 1 (Dec 1-7):   Stock 2x normal → Price normal
├─ Week 2 (Dec 8-14):  Stock 3x normal → Price +10%
├─ Week 3 (Dec 15-21): Stock 4x normal → Price normal
└─ Week 4 (Dec 22-25): PEAK - Discount 10-20%
```

### Medium Multiplier Periods (1.5x - 1.8x)
```
Back to School, Weddings, Navratri
├─ 2 weeks before: Stock 1.5x, Price normal
├─ 1 week before: Stock 2x, Price normal
└─ during Festival: Discount 5-10%, clear inventory
```

### Low/Off Seasons (1.0x - 1.3x)
```
JULY, APRIL, JUNE
├─ Stock normal or reduce
├─ Run clearance for older inventory
└─ Use for planning next peak season
```
