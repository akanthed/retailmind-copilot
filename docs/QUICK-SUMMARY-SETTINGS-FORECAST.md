- **Purpose**: Get real-time alerts 14 days before festivals

## 2️⃣ Festival Peaks Research for India

### Major Peaks in 2026:

#### 🔴 **Diwali (Nov 4)** - HIGHEST PEAK
- **Impact**: 2.5x normal demand
- **Duration**: 6+ weeks (Oct 1 - Nov 30)
- **Best categories**: Electronics, Jewelry, Clothing, Home Decor
- **Strategy**: Stock 3-4x normal inventory starting Oct 1

#### 🟡 **Holi (Mar 14)** - MAJOR PEAK
- **Impact**: 2.1x normal demand  
- **Pre-peak**: Mar 1-13 (1.9x)
- **Best categories**: Colors, Food, Sweets, Clothing
- **Strategy**: Stock 2-3x from Mar 1

#### 🟠 **Christmas (Dec 25)** - MAJOR PEAK
- **Impact**: 2.2x normal demand
- **Duration**: 4 weeks (Dec 1-24)
- **Best categories**: Electronics, Toys, Gifts, Food
- **Strategy**: Build inventory from Nov 15

#### 🟡 Other Significant Peaks:
- **Wedding Season** (Jan + May): 1.7-1.8x → Jewelry, Fashion
- **Navratri** (Sep): 1.7-1.8x → Clothing, Religious items
- **Back to School** (May): 1.6x → Electronics, Books
- **New Year** (Jan 1): 1.6x → Electronics, Fashion
- **Black Friday** (Nov 25): 1.8x → Everything

### Why These Peaks Matter:
1. **Diwali**: Indian traditional festival - gives gifts, buys electronics, jewelry
2. **Holi**: Festival of colors - clothing, sweets, festival items
3. **Christmas**: Global shopping season + religious celebration
4. **Weddings**: Peak season in India (Jan-Feb, May-Jun)

### Demand Pattern Throughout the Year:
```
Q1 (Jan-Mar): Wedding season + Holi peak → 1.5x average
Q2 (Apr-Jun): Back to school + Wedding season → 1.5x average
Q3 (Jul-Sep): Navratri prep → 1.4x average, low in Jul
Q4 (Oct-Dec): 🔴 DIWALI SUPERCYCLE → 2.0x+ average
```

## 3️⃣ How the Forecast Works

### Calculation Formula:
```
Daily Demand = Base Demand × Festival Impact × Weekend Multiplier × AI Adjustment
```

**Example for Diwali**:
- Base demand: 10 units/day
- Diwali multiplier: 2.5x
- If it's a Saturday: 1.2x
- AI adjustment: 1.0x
- **Result**: 10 × 2.5 × 1.2 × 1.0 = **30 units on Diwali Saturday** (vs 10 normally)

### What the Forecast Shows:
- **30-day daily predictions** with confidence scores
- **Festival markers** showing which days have peaks
- **Peak periods** listed with expected demand
- **Stockout warnings** if inventory runs low
- **AI-powered recommendations** based on market conditions

### Confidence Score (0.6-0.95):
- Higher = more historical data available
- Lower = new products or unusual conditions
- Decreases for dates further in future
- Lower during festival periods (inherent volatility)

## 💡 Key Insights

### Highest Opportunity Periods
1. **Oct 21 - Nov 15** (Diwali pre-peak): 1.9-2.5x demand
2. **Mar 1-20** (Holi peak): 1.9-2.1x demand
3. **Dec 1-25** (Christmas): 1.7-2.2x demand

### Quick Wins
- **Every Saturday/Sunday**: +20% demand vs weekdays (1.2x multiplier)
- **Multiple festivals same month**: Demand compounds (e.g., Aug has Rakhi + Independence Day)
- **Post-festival**: Highest discount period for clearance

### Critical Metrics to Monitor
1. **Days Until Stockout**: If < 7 days before festival = urgent restock needed
2. **Live URL Health**: Keep >95% working URLs for accurate price tracking
3. **Confidence Score**: >0.8 = reliable forecast, <0.75 = add safety margin

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Which festival has the biggest impact?**
A: Diwali (2.5x) in Nov, followed by Christmas (2.2x) in Dec, then Holi (2.1x) in Mar

**Q: When should I start stocking for Diwali?**
A: Oct 1 (earliest is Oct 1, critical is Oct 21+)

**Q: What if URL validation shows all down?**
A: Check internet connection, then verify products still exist on Amazon/Flipkart

**Q: How often does the forecast update?**
A: Daily, based on latest sales data and real-time pricing

**Q: Are offline festivals (like regional ones) included?**
A: Only major national festivals shown. Regional variations need manual adjustment

**Q: What if a festival affects multiple categories?**
A: Forecast applies multiplier to ALL matching categories automatically
