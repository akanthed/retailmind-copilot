# Product Overview

RetailMind AI is an AI-powered market intelligence platform for small and mid-sized retailers. It provides competitive pricing analysis, demand forecasting, and conversational business insights to help retailers make data-driven decisions.

## Core Features

- AI Copilot powered by Amazon Bedrock Nova Pro for natural language business queries
- Product management with competitor price tracking
- Price monitoring and historical trend analysis
- Recommendation engine for pricing and inventory decisions
- Alert system for market condition changes
- Analytics dashboard with revenue impact tracking

## Target Users

Small and mid-sized retailers who need enterprise-level intelligence at an affordable price point ($0.42/month per retailer).

## Business Value

- 80% time savings on pricing research
- Proactive alerts to prevent stockouts
- 5-15% potential revenue increase
- Data-driven decisions replacing guesswork
- Competitive intelligence previously unavailable to small retailers

## Data Sources

The system supports both real and synthetic data sources for hackathon/demo purposes, with clear indicators of data source types.
